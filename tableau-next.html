Tableau Next

Agentic analytics that delivers personalized, contextual, and actionable insights everywhere work happens.

The future of analytics has arrived. Meet Tableau Next.

Tableau Next is a flexible, API-first analytics experience that integrates the #1 analytics platform with Agentforce, the world’s first digital labor platform. Built on a composable architecture with a unified data layer and trusted semantics, it delivers personalized and actionable insights to every user.

Tableau Next. Analytics, now with Agentforce.

Tableau Next is now available via Tableau+. Experience the future of data and analytics today.

Unlock the power of agentic AI

Tableau Semantics is an AI-infused semantic layer deeply integrated with Data 360, enabling trusted and unified business data.

Turn data into insights faster

Natively integrated with Agentforce, Tableau Next enables proactive insights, natural language queries, and 24/7 analytics agents.

Explore, visualize, and act on data directly within workflows with built-in actionability across the platform.
