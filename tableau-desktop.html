Simplify unlimited data exploration

From data preparation to consumption, Tableau Desktop gives you everything you need to deliver real-time insights. Ask new questions, spot trends, identify opportunities, and make data-informed decisions with confidence.

Expedite business results with intelligent insights you can trust. Transparent agentic capabilities help democratize what-if scenario planning, guided model building, and AI-powered predictions.
